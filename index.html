My Work

Le Petit Pets: UX/UI Web Responsive App

Le Petit Pets would be a recommendation location-based responsive app that would assist the user to find a pet-sitter, have drop in visits, dog walking, and onboarding. Unlike other pet service app that are already in the market like Rover and Wag, these services would extend past just dogs and cats but to reptiles, rabbits and birds. Functionalities would include setting up a profile for your pet and the owner, tracking your pet, and personalized search of pet provider.

Awaken: UX/UI Native App

For this project as the UX/UI designer for this project the brief was to select an app concept of my choosing that would solve a specific user problem, the only requirement was to design for both iOS and Android and native.The solution to this problem was to develop a mental health care app that would be accessible to everyone. The name of this app would be called Awaken. They will be able to select what type of therapy they are looking for (depression, anxiety, anger, stress, relationship, grief, self-esteem, eating disorder, borderline personality disorder) they will have the option of having a private mode or public when receiving therapy.

Ithaka: UX/UI Web Responsive App

This is my most recent project, the brief was very specific for this. My role for this project was to be the UX/UI designer for either an online clothing store or investment app, that had to be web responsive. I decided to go with an online store that sells ethical and sustainable clothes and home essentials - ITHAKA. The user stories were already selected and I had to create the screens for these specific needs/wants from the user. The problem that the app is solving is for is for people who want to shop for key items without going into a physical store. This online store is particularly valuable for people who struggle to find items they want in a traditional stores.
